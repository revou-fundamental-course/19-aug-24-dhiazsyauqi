Vision

Mission

To organize and develop world-class education with
entrepreneurial mindset.

To develop and disseminate new knowledge and intellectual
properties in the fields of technology, science, and art that
contribute to the fulfillment of sustainable development goals.

To collaborate with industries and stakeholders in the
development of innovations that contribute to the national
economic growth.
